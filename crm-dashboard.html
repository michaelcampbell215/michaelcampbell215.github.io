CRM Dashboard: Sales Performance Analysis

An interactive Tableau dashboard to track pipeline metrics, benchmark team performance, and optimize the sales funnel.

Objective

To develop a comprehensive dashboard that provides sales leadership with a clear view of the sales pipeline, identifies bottlenecks, and highlights high-performing agents and products.

Tools Used

Dashboard Key Features

Pipeline Overview

  • Funnel Metrics: Visualized the sales funnel from Prospecting → Engaging → Won/Lost
  • Conversion Rates: 94% of prospecting deals progressed to Engaging, but only 51% were ultimately won
  • Cycle Time Analysis: Calculated average deal cycle length (48 days), identifying the fastest and slowest closers

Sales Team Performance

  • Performance by Region, Manager, and Sales Agent: Built side-by-side comparisons and dynamic filters to benchmark performance at all levels
  • Top Performer Spotlight: Darcel Schlecht outperformed peers by 60%, influencing Central region success under manager Melvin Marxen
  • Win Rate & Time-to-Close Metrics: Evaluated efficiency alongside revenue impact

Product & Pricing Analysis

  • Revenue by Product & Sector: GTX Pro led sales, while MG Special underperformed
  • Pricing Insight: Created a calculated field to compare actual close price vs. list price to detect markups or discounts
  • Elasticity View: Built a scatter plot showing product level pricing behavior across sectors
  • Sector Preference Mapping: Visualized which products performed best in different industries

Key Business Insights

  • Q2 drove the highest revenue ($3.09M), while Q4 lagged despite similar volume suggesting pricing issues
  • MG Special and GTX Plus Pro were the only products closed above targeted list price, hinting at value or positioning gaps in other products
  • Bottleneck detected in late funnel conversions, warranting improved negotiation training or support
  • Strong correlation between agent/manager pairing and region level success

Recommendations

  • Investigate Q4 discounts and re-evaluate pricing strategies for underperforming products
  • Replicate high performing agent behaviors (e.g., Darcel’s approach) across regions
  • Provide training support for managers with low performing teams
  • Implement more rigorous CRM data entry practices to avoid gaps in funnel reporting

Tools & Techniques

  • Tableau: Advanced dashboard interactivity using parameters, filters and calculated fields
  • Excel: Initial data cleaning and preparation
  • Data Storytelling: Designed with stakeholders in mind executives, regional managers, and product leads
