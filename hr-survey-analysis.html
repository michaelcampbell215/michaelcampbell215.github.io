HR Survey Analysis

An Excel based analysis of employee survey data to identify key drivers of satisfaction and guide HR strategy.

Objectives

  • Assess overall employee satisfaction based on survey responses.
  • Identify key areas where employees feel supported and valued.
  • Pinpoint areas of dissatisfaction to guide HR strategies for improvement.

Tools Used

Excel Power Query

Data Source

HR Dataset

Employee Survey Data from the Seattle Department of Public Health.

Related Industries

Human Resources People Analytics Corporate Management

Process

  • Data Cleaning: Identified and rectified missing values and duplicate entries. Normalized textual data by removing trailing spaces and correcting inconsistencies using Power Query.
  • Data Visualization & Analysis: Created visual representations in Excel, including a 100% stacked chart to highlight trends in satisfaction responses. Analyzed response distributions to quantitatively determine strengths and areas for improvement.

Key Findings

Positive Insights:

  • Employees had a clear understanding of their job expectations.
  • Respondents felt supported by their supervisors and coworkers.

Areas for Improvement:

  • Many employees did not feel appreciated for their work.
  • A lack of connection among coworkers was a notable concern.

Recommendations

  • Implement employee recognition programs to improve appreciation.
  • Foster team-building activities to strengthen workplace relationships.
  • Conduct follow-up surveys to track progress and refine engagement strategies.
