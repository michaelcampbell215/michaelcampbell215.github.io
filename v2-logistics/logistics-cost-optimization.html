Key Insights

Cost Concentration

ARV and HRDT groups account for 99% of total logistics spend ($1.46B+), identifying the primary levers for cost reduction.

Mode Reliability

Ocean shipments (84% OTD) and standard Air (88% OTD) contribute most to delays compared to 100% OTD for Air Charter.

Lead Time Variance

Average lead times varied from 88 days (Air) to 144 days (Ocean), critical for inventory replenishment planning.

Strategic Recommendation

"Shift logistics focus from global sweeping changes to targeted negotiations for Air and Ocean freight on high-volume ARV routes to capture immediate cost savings."
