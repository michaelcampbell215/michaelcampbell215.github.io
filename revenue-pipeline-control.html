Building Operational Clarity

01

Funnel Bottleneck Analysis

Visualized the transition from prospecting to close using LOD expressions to calculate true stage-by-stage conversion rates — not simple opportunity counts. Identified a 49% drop-off concentrated specifically in the Proposal-to-Negotiation transition, pinpointing proposal quality as the bottleneck rather than prospecting volume.

02

Agent Benchmarking

Engineered dynamic agent and region filters to isolate top performers — the highest-performing rep outperformed the company baseline by 60%. Used behavior patterns from the top cohort to define an "Ideal Closing Script" and surface the specific stage transitions where laggard reps were losing deals.

03

Pricing Elasticity Tracking

Developed calculated fields to compare actual close prices against target ASPs. Revealed value positioning gaps in product lines being systematically discounted in Q4 — a pattern invisible in flat revenue reporting but visible the moment price integrity is tracked as a calculated field alongside volume.

Sales Optimization

  • • Replicate top-performer stage transition behaviors across laggard regions using the benchmarking filter as a coaching tool.
  • • Investigate Q4 discount triggers to protect quarterly margins — the pricing elasticity view identifies which product lines are most exposed.

Product Strategy

  • • Focus pipeline investment on the GTX Pro line due to superior price integrity and deal velocity relative to comparable products.
  • • Re-evaluate underperforming product positioning in segments where win rate consistently trails the portfolio average.
