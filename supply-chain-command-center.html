Command Center Insights

01

Logistics Optimization

Geographic mapping of physical unit routing revealed that San Diego drives significantly higher surgical device throughput than Los Angeles, defying traditional population-based assumptions. This provided grounds to restructure the West Coast Forward Stocking Locations to minimize true "last mile" transit times.

02

Sales Cyclicality & The Whale Curve

Constructed Lorenz curves distinguishing a "hockey stick" culture of massive end-of-quarter volume pushes followed by drop-offs. The concentration index showed that over 80% of revenue heavily relies on just the TOP 2% active accounts, triggering an overhaul in target incentive structuring.

03

Compliance Exposure Tracking

Applying Z-Score thresholds in the raw SQL allowed us to mathematically isolate anomalous discretionary spending safely. This triggered a major audit of $11.7M in Unallocated consulting fees grouped alongside an unexpected $170M+ anomaly event.
