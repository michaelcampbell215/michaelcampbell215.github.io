Global Supply Chain Performance & Cost Optimization

An interactive dashboard to monitor global logistics, identify underperforming shipments, and support cost negotiations.

Objective

To design and implement an analytical tool for a global logistics team to provide insights into delivery performance and cost efficiency.

Tools Used

Tableau Python SQL

Data Source

SCMS_Delivery_History_Dataset

Related Industries

Data Cleaning and Transformation (Python/Pandas)

Performed extensive data wrangling and feature engineering using Python (Pandas) to prepare the raw data for analysis. This involved:

  • Converting date fields to datetime objects and handling "Date Not Captured" entries.
  • Converting Weight (Kilograms) and Freight Cost (USD) to numeric types, addressing non-numeric values and imputing NULLs with 0.
  • Filling missing Line Item Insurance (USD) values with 0 and Shipment Mode with "Unknown".

Engineered Key Features

  • Created Delivery Lead Time (Days) and a binary On-Time Delivery Flag (1 if on-time, 0 if late).
  • Calculated Total Logistics Cost, Freight Cost % of Line Item Value, and Cost Per Unit Shipped.

Reporting & Dashboard Development (Tableau)

Designed and developed two interactive, user-friendly dashboards in Tableau, translating complex raw data and calculated metrics into intuitive visualizations. Utilized filters and dashboard actions to enable dynamic exploration and ad-hoc analysis.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

  • Delivery Performance: Overall On-Time Delivery (OTD) Rate, OTD by Country/Shipment Mode/Vendor, Average Delivery Lead Time.
  • Cost Efficiency: Total Logistics Cost (by Country/Product Group/Shipment Mode/Vendor), Freight Cost as % of Line Item Value, Cost Per Unit Shipped.

Findings & Insights

  • Overall OTD: Revealed an overall On Time Delivery Rate of 88.51%, indicating strong general performance but also a clear opportunity to improve the remaining 11.49% of late deliveries.
  • Geographical Performance: Identified varying OTD rates across countries, highlighting regions with consistent on-time performance (e.g., South Sudan, Sudan often at 100%) and those requiring attention.
  • Shipment Mode Impact: Showed that Air Charter and Truck modes had 100% OTD, while Air (88.89%) and Ocean (84.34%) modes contributed most to delays.
  • Lead Time Variance: Revealed average lead times varied significantly by mode (e.g., Air 88 days vs. Ocean 144 days), crucial for planning and expectation management.
  • Cost Distribution: Analysis of Total Logistics Cost demonstrated that ARV and HRDT product groups account for over 99% of total logistics spend (ARV alone at $1.46 Billion), directing focus to these high-impact categories for cost-saving initiatives.

Recommendations

    Re-evaluate Shipment Mode Strategies:

    Reasoning: The data shows that while Air Charter and Truck have perfect OTD rates, the lower-performing Ocean and Air modes are the primary contributors to late deliveries.

    Action: Conduct a cost-benefit analysis for each shipment mode on a per-route basis to determine if shifting more volume to faster, more reliable modes is a worthwhile investment to avoid delays.


    Initiate a Targeted Cost-Reduction Initiative:

    Reasoning: The analysis found that ARV and HRDT product groups account for over 99% of total logistics costs.

    Action: Focus cost reduction efforts on these two product groups first. Perform a deeper dive into these specific product lines to understand cost drivers, such as analyzing the cost per unit by country or vendor to find additional areas for savings.
